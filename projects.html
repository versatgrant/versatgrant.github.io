SimplePaint.vb

SimplePaint.vb is a visual basic graphical application that I submitted as my final project for the Visual Basic course at Monroe College. For the most part, the code was taken from TechnologyUK.net and I added the simple functionality of loading background images instead of starting with a blank canvas.

For my Systems Analysis final project, I, along with my team, planned the implementation of an ordering system for a bronx based Mexican restaurant. Operating Systems Research Paper

For this research paper, my group and I discussed in detail, the functional operations of the iOS 9 operating system. We focused on the logical processes of the sub-managers that make up the operating system (Memory, Processor, File, Device, Network) and its unique user-interface.
